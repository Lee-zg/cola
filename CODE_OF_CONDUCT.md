# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and constructive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them.
- Prefer clear technical reasoning over personal preference.
- Help maintain a productive environment for contributors and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Any conduct that maintainers reasonably determine to be inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other action when behavior harms the project community.

Report concerns privately through the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md` when public discussion is not appropriate.
